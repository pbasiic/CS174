Personal

Sophia Singh was born September 6, 2002 in Dallas, Texas. She was born a child prodigy who had an innate skill for reading people's minds and emotions. Her powers quickly developed from a young age as she was trained by her mentor, Master Yoda. She eventually became the Yoda's smartest student with unmatched telepathic skills. During her training with Yoda, she met Luke Skywalker and they banded forces. Together, the two were unstoppable and quickly became Yoda's most gifted students. Skywalker vowed to protect Sophia from any harm and promised he would arrive if she ever were in trouble. Long after their training, Skywalker has kept true to that promise by saving Sophia from the vicious evils of sorority girls.

Professional

Sophia currently attends San Jose State University and is completing her last semester as a computer science student. She is looking to expand her career and go into software engineering or project management once her degree has been completed. Sophia has worked on several projects over her term, her favorite being a 2D platformer game called Wisps that she published in 2022. Another project Sophia worked on was for a computer networks class, she created a three tiered network using RIP and OSPF protocols for Activision Blizzard's company model.
